About ToolsForge

Mission

ToolsForge is dedicated to providing free, open-source tools for document and media manipulation. We believe that powerful productivity tools should be accessible to everyone, without hidden costs or subscriptions.

What We Offer

From PDF editing and image processing to text conversion and QR code generation, ToolsForge provides a comprehensive suite of productivity tools. All tools are powered by cutting-edge open-source libraries.

Technology

Built with Next.js, React, and TypeScript. Powered by pdf.js, pdf-lib, Tesseract.js, and other open-source technologies. Designed to be fast, reliable, and completely private.

Privacy First

Your files never leave your browser. All processing happens client-side. No tracking, no analytics, no data collection. Complete privacy and control over your documents.

Questions? Check out our documentation, browse the FAQ, or contact us directly. We're here to help make your workflow smoother.
